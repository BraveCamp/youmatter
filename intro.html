This is your first scenario: You will take the role of an Asian-American woman.

You are a CEO, with an appearance younger than 30. Your goal is to interact with people in a business conference and experience the challenges minority women face when doing the same jobs as men. After you interact with a person you will be given the option to continue in the same scenario, move on to the next, or simply exit the game. Feel free to exit the game anytime simply by clicking "Exit Game" in the upper-left hand corner of your screen. Click the picture to start your first interaction

Approach a person to introduce yourself.

Disclaimer: The goal of our game is to educate what potential situations people part of the minority group go through. We are not making representations for minority groups.
